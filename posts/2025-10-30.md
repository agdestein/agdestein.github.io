# Make Beamer slides 16:9

[Beamer](https://www.overleaf.com/learn/latex/Beamer)
is an ergonomic tool to generate slides from raw text files with LaTeX.
A minimal example is
```latex
\documentclass{beamer}

\begin{document}

\begin{frame}
    Hello!
\end{frame}

\end{document}
```

By default, Beamer slides have an aspect ratio of 4:3, while
most modern projectors and screens have the 16:9 aspect ratio.
To fill the entire screen, a simple modification is to add the `aspectratio=169` option
to the documentclass:
```latex
\documentclass[aspectratio=169]{beamer}
```

Microsoft Powerpoint and Google Slides both set the aspect ratio to 16:9 by default.
Beamer users should do the same.
